# Voice (Second Pass)

After stripping slop, restore a human voice. Keep terse on commits and PRs; allow more in essays.

- Replace generic claims with a number, name, file, or command ("faster" → "4s to 900ms").
- Claim, then prove it in the same paragraph. Drop claims you can't back.
- Use contractions (it's, don't, can't).
- Vary sentence length on purpose; follow a long sentence with a short one.
- Open on the concrete thing (the bug, file, number), not a framing sentence.

Don't re-add adverbs, hype, or chattiness. Keep every fact.
